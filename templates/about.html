E-Governance Complaint & Service Management Portal

This portal helps citizens raise complaints online, track progress clearly, and receive updates without unnecessary office visits. It is designed for faster communication between citizens and the administration.

24x7 Online access
Live Status tracking
Secure User and admin access
Purpose

Why this portal was created

Traditional complaint handling can take time because citizens often need to visit offices, submit papers, and wait for manual updates. This portal keeps the process online, organized, and easier to follow. Citizens can submit a complaint, admins can manage it, and everyone can see the latest status.

Features

What citizens and admins can do

01

Online Complaint Submission

Citizens can submit complaint details and supporting files from the portal.

02

Real-Time Status Tracking

Users can check whether a complaint is pending, in progress, or resolved.

03

Role-Based Access

Citizens and administrators get separate access based on their work.

04

Admin Dashboard

Admins can review complaints, update status, and manage user feedback.

Workflow

Simple complaint process

1

Register or login

Create an account or login to access complaint services.

2

Submit complaint

Add the issue, category, location, description, and file if needed.

3

Track updates

Follow status changes from the dashboard until the complaint is resolved.
