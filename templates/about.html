E-Governance Complaint & Service Management Portal

A digital platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens and government, making complaint management transparent, efficient and accountable.

🎯 Purpose of This Portal

This portal was built to replace the traditional manual complaint handling system with a modern digital platform. Citizens can now register their complaints online, track their status in real-time, and get timely resolutions without visiting government offices physically.

⭐ Key Features

📝

Online Complaint Submission

Citizens can submit complaints from anywhere, anytime.

🔍

Real-Time Status Tracking

Track the progress of your complaint at every stage.

🛡️

Role-Based Access Control

Separate secure access for citizens and administrators.

📊

Admin Dashboard

Admins can view, manage and resolve all complaints efficiently.
