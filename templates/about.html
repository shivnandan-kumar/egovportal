Our Purpose

Empowering Citizens Through Digital Governance

This portal is designed to make public grievance handling faster, more transparent, and more accessible. It helps citizens connect with government departments through a structured complaint management process.

Easy Complaint Registration

Citizens can submit complaints online with relevant details and supporting documents.

Real-Time Tracking

Users can monitor complaint status and stay updated at every stage of the process.

Transparent Governance

Every complaint follows a clear workflow, improving visibility and accountability.

Timely Resolution

Departments can review, prioritize, and resolve complaints more efficiently.

Citizen-Centric Services

The platform improves convenience by reducing the need for physical office visits.
